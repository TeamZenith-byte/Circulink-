❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI waste classification work?+
CIRCULINK uses Google Gemini 1.5 Flash Vision API to analyze your uploaded waste photo. It identifies the waste category, quality grade (0–100), and estimates a fair market price in INR per kg — all within 2–3 seconds.
Is listing waste on CIRCULINK free?+
Yes! Listing waste is completely free. CIRCULINK is a community platform designed to connect sellers and buyers without any commission in the current version.
How do I get paid after a buyer places an order?+
When a buyer places an order, our admin team reviews it and contacts both parties via phone or email to arrange the pickup and payment. We're currently working on UPI/Razorpay integration for Version 2.1.
What types of waste does CIRCULINK support?+
We support 8 major waste categories: Plastic, Paper & Cardboard, Metal, E-Waste, Glass, Organic, Textile, and Rubber. More categories will be added in future versions.
How are NGO donations handled?+
When you submit a donation, our admin team forwards your details to the selected NGO partner. The NGO will contact you within 24 hours to arrange item pickup or dropoff.
