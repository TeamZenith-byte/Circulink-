How CIRCULINK Works

From waste photo to cash in your pocket — the entire process takes under 3 minutes.

1
📸
Upload Waste Photo

Take a photo of your recyclable waste and upload it on the platform. Any waste type works.

2
🤖
AI Classifies It

Google Gemini Vision AI identifies category, quality score (0–100), and suggests fair price in seconds.

3
📋
List in Marketplace

One click to publish your listing. Verified buyers across India can see it instantly.

4
💰
Get Paid

Buyers place orders or make offers. Admin connects both parties to complete the deal.
